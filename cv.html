Curriculum Vitae

Bachelor of Science

My BSc. studies took place at UCT Prague between the years 2014-2017 with the area of study being general chemistry. Here I first got in contact with molecular simulations in the group of prof. Pavel Jungwirth at IOCB Prague working in a biophysical project concerning insulin crystallization states. In 2017, I defended my theis "Interaction of Arginine with Insulin and Effect on its Aggregation" and graduated with honors.

Master of Science

My MSc. studies directly followed in 2017-2019 at UCT Prague (physical chemistry) with research done with prof. Pavel Jungwirth at IOCB Prague. At this moment I started working with ab initio molecular dynamics, simulating the behavior of solvated electrons in liquid ammonia and their application in reduction of aromatic coupounds in a process known in organic chemistry as the Birch reduction. In 2019, I defended my thesis called "Quantum Chemistry Calculations of Solvated Electrons in the Context of the Birch Reduction" and graduated with honors.

Doctoral Studies

Since October 2019, I am following my doctoral study program at the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, Charles University in Prague under the advisorship of dr. Ondrej Marsalek as documented on this website.
