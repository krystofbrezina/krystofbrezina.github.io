Extracurricular Activities and Interests

Before fully committing my time to science, I used to study piano at the Prague Conservatoire (2015-2017) parallel to my university studies. Alongside concerts, I participated in several competitions on the national and international level, resulting notably in the First prize award from the Valsesia Musica Junior competition in Italy (June 2016). Until now, I continue with my playing on a "semi-professional" level (look for possible updates here). I also cherish classical literature and poetry (I am a huge fan of Dante) and visual arts. Sometimes however, I find it necessary to clean out my mind and in those moment I find refuge in some sports (weight lifting and swimming) and travelling.
