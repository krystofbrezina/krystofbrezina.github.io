Hi,

welcome to my website. I'm a PhD student in the group of Ondrej Marsalek at Charles University in Prague, Czech republic. I take part in research in an area of science at the borders of theoretical physics, chemistry and computational technology—molecular simulation. Here, we use advanced theoretical approaches to study electronic and molecular structure in hydrogen-bonded liquid systems. I made this website to share my (mostly) work-related news, outlooks and achievements, so feel free to look around and see what I'm up to.
